Bryan Espinoza-Prieto

Ph.D. Student in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology

University of Tennessee, Knoxville (Aug 2025 – present)

I am a botanist specializing in the taxonomy and ecology of liverworts, particularly Frullania (Frullaniaceae, Marchantiophyta). My research focuses on the distribution, floristics, and ecological variation of bryophytes across Peru.
