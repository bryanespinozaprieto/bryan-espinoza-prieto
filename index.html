Brief Research Overview

My research has focused on the taxonomy of Frullania and bryophyte floristics in Peru. Currently, my work focuses on resolving species complexes, evolutionary history, and distribution of this genus in the Neotropics. I combine fieldwork, herbarium studies, and genomic approaches to delimit species, infer phylogenies, and study evolutionary patterns in the region.
