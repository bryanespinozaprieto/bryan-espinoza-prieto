Hello! I am Bryan Espinoza-Prieto, a researcher focused on bryophytes, especially the genus Frullania. My work spans taxonomy, floristics, and ecological distribution studies in Peru, including Andean forests, Amazonian ecosystems, and coastal zones.

In this website, you can find my CV, publications, field photos, and information on Peru's vegetation types.
