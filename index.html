Hello! I am Bryan Espinoza Prieto, a Peruvian botanist specializing in bryophytes, particularly the genus Frullania. I am currently a Ph.D. Student in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville (Aug 2025 – present). My research interests lie at the intersection of taxonomy, floristics, and biogeography of liverworts in Peru.
